**Flame:** Focuses on aggressive attacks, explosions, and burning damage over time. Flame weapons include blazing swords, magma flails, fire spears, explosive gauntlets, flamethrowers, ember bows, and volcanic launchers. Attacks can ignite enemies, spread fire across the battlefield, and trigger powerful explosions when burning targets are struck repeatedly.

**Verdance:** Channels the ancient magic of forests, storms, enchanted flora, and wild spirits. Verdance weapons include thorn whips, spirit bows, crescent sickles, blossom staffs, lightning rods, thunder drums, vine-wrapped spears, fungal lanterns, and stormcaller branches. Attacks can entangle enemies with roots, summon spectral animals, release glowing spores, call down lightning, create violent storms, and transform the battlefield into a magical overgrown grove.

**Terrene:** Commands stone, crystal, metal, clay, and sand through the ancient magic beneath the world. Terrene weapons include sandstone hammers, crystal axes, obsidian blades, sand whips, seismic gauntlets, desert spears, boulder launchers, and floating stone rings. Attacks can raise pillars, create earthquakes, summon sandstorms, trap enemies in sinking ground, and shape earth into armor, walls, blades, or temporary guardians.

**Gale:** Emphasizes speed, aerial movement, knockback, and rapid attacks. Gale weapons include war fans, chakrams, boomerangs, wind bows, lightweight spears, cyclone blades, air cannons, and feather daggers. Attacks can launch enemies into the air, redirect projectiles, create tornadoes, and allow the player to dash, glide, or remain airborne during combat.

**Arcane:** Manipulates raw magical energy, runes, portals, stars, and reality itself. Arcane weapons include wands, spellbooks, floating orbs, rune blades, magic cards, crystal prisms, portal staffs, and enchanted mirrors. These weapons consume mana and can fire homing energy, open portals, create illusions, bend space, or produce stronger effects when spells are cast in specific combinations.

**Shadow:** Uses darkness, curses, stealth, illusions, and life-draining attacks. Shadow weapons include daggers, scythes, shadow chains, cursed bows, spectral claws, dark lanterns, and crescent blades. Attacks can mark enemies with curses, create shadow clones, teleport through darkness, drain health, and deal increased damage when striking from behind or attacking unseen.
