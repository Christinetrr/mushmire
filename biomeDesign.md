# Five New Surface Biomes

## ❄️ Frostveil Peaks

A towering mountain realm covered in silver snow, frozen waterfalls, crystal caves, and ancient wind shrines. Snow drifts reshape the landscape during storms, while warm springs create small pockets of glowing life between the cliffs.

**Resources:** frost crystal, silverwood, cloud wool, ice lotus
**Enemies:** Frost Moths, Snow Spirits, Icebound Guardians, Mountain Wisps
**Landmarks:** frozen monasteries, abandoned observatories, hot springs, rope bridges
**Special mechanics:** slippery surfaces, blizzards, falling icicles, temporary snow bridges

---

## 🌫 Whispering Fen

A magical wetland filled with enormous lilies, glowing reeds, crooked trees, drifting fog, and half-submerged ruins. At night, the swamp seems alive: lights move beneath the water, trees whisper, and pathways quietly change.

**Resources:** ghost reeds, bog iron, lotus silk, witchwood
**Enemies:** Lantern Frogs, Bog Mimics, Mire Hags, Reed Serpents
**Landmarks:** stilt villages, sunken shrines, giant lily fields, abandoned ferries
**Special mechanics:** deep mud, poisonous water pockets, floating lily platforms, shifting fog

---

## 🌋 Emberwild

A volcanic forest where black-barked trees grow glowing red leaves and rivers of molten crystal flow through the earth. Ash falls gently like snow, creating a beautiful but dangerous landscape shaped by fire.

**Resources:** emberwood, obsidian, volcanic glass, fire petals
**Enemies:** Cinder Foxes, Ash Wraiths, Magma Beetles, Ember Toads
**Landmarks:** forge temples, lava gardens, ruined settlements, volcano craters
**Special mechanics:** rising heat, lava bursts, burning terrain, fire-resistant plants

---

## 🌙 Umbral Thicket

A forest trapped in permanent twilight, filled with silver trees, mirror-like ponds, black flowers, and shadows that move independently of their owners. The biome is eerie rather than evil, hiding rare creatures and forgotten moon temples.

**Resources:** duskwood, moonstone, shadow silk, night blossoms
**Enemies:** Hollow Deer, Shade Stalkers, Moonless Owls, Mirror Wraiths
**Landmarks:** ruined manors, moon gates, mirror ponds, hidden shadow paths
**Special mechanics:** false platforms, moving shadows, temporary invisibility zones, darkness portals

---

## 🌠 Starfall Highlands

A high-altitude region formed around ancient meteor impacts. Pieces of earth float above glowing craters, gravity behaves strangely, and the grass shines beneath a sky filled with falling stars.

**Resources:** starstone, celestial metal, comet glass, cloud fiber
**Enemies:** Astral Moths, Orbiting Sentinels, Starborn Slimes, Comet Hounds
**Landmarks:** meteor craters, floating ruins, ancient telescopes, broken sky bridges
**Special mechanics:** low-gravity zones, floating terrain, falling star events, gravity-switching puzzles

# Three Underground Biomes

## 🍄 Mycelial Deep

A vast fungal kingdom beneath the Verdant Tropics and Whispering Fen. Giant mushrooms form forests underground, glowing spores drift through the air, and ancient remnants of the original Mush Empire are buried throughout the caverns.

**Resources:** luminous caps, spore silk, mycelium stone, royal fungus
**Enemies:** Spore Knights, Puffcaps, Fungal Crawlers, Mushroom Mimics
**Landmarks:** mushroom cathedrals, buried throne rooms, spore rivers, forgotten villages
**Special mechanics:** bouncy mushrooms, spreading fungal terrain, spore clouds, living cave walls

---

## 💎 Crystal Hollows

A deep cavern network filled with enormous geodes, reflective crystal lakes, mineral plants, and creatures made from living stone. Light refracts through the caverns, revealing hidden passages and activating ancient machinery.

**Resources:** prism crystal, geode fragments, echo stone, crystal resin
**Enemies:** Shard Beetles, Crystal Golems, Prism Bats, Gem Serpents
**Landmarks:** giant geodes, crystal bridges, buried mines, reflecting temples
**Special mechanics:** beam-reflection puzzles, breakable crystal floors, growing crystals, mirrored projectiles

---

## 🏺 Buried Kingdom

An enormous sandstone civilization hidden beneath the Sunken Dunes. Entire streets, palaces, gardens, and tombs remain sealed beneath layers of shifting sand.

**Resources:** ancient gold, sunstone, royal sandstone, scarab shell
**Enemies:** Tomb Wardens, Gilded Mummies, Sand Wyrms, Cursed Statues
**Landmarks:** royal vaults, underground pyramids, collapsed marketplaces, sealed tombs
**Special mechanics:** flowing sand, pressure traps, collapsing rooms, rotating stone doors

# Three Underwater and Sea Biomes

## 🪸 Moonreef Gardens

A luminous coral forest found beneath the Glass Ocean. Coral branches glow in changing colors, schools of spirit fish move through the water, and giant pearl flowers open only at night.

**Resources:** moon coral, pearl petals, reef glass, luminous scales
**Enemies:** Reef Sirens, Coral Crawlers, Moonfish, Blooming Anemones
**Landmarks:** coral palaces, pearl gardens, sunken statues, giant shell chambers
**Special mechanics:** healing coral currents, bioluminescent paths, retracting anemones, tide-powered doors

---

## 📚 Drowned Archives

A submerged network of libraries, laboratories, and magical study halls lost beneath the sea. Books remain preserved inside bubbles, while unfinished experiments still wander through flooded corridors.

**Resources:** sealed scrolls, arcane ink, bubble glass, drowned crystal
**Enemies:** Ink Wraiths, Bookfish, Archive Guardians, Spellbound Eels
**Landmarks:** underwater libraries, flooded laboratories, bubble chambers, broken observatories
**Special mechanics:** air-pocket puzzles, water-level controls, magical locks, collectible spell records

---

## 🌑 The Abyssal Veil

A deep-sea trench far below the Glass Ocean where sunlight cannot reach. Strange glowing creatures drift through the darkness, enormous bones cover the seabed, and ancient structures emerge from the black water.

**Resources:** abyss pearl, void coral, deepstone, leviathan bone
**Enemies:** Veil Anglers, Bone Eels, Abyssal Watchers, Deepwater Wraiths
**Landmarks:** leviathan skeletons, trench temples, thermal vents, sealed abyss gates
**Special mechanics:** crushing-depth zones, limited visibility, glowing trails, powerful underwater currents
