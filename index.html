✦ Traveler's Manual ✦

Moving Around

◀ ▶ — walk  ·  double-tap ◀ or ▶ and hold — sprint

⬆ — jump  ·  tap ⬆ again mid-air — double jump

⬇ — duck  ·  double-tap ⬇ — roll (dodges enemy hits!)

Touching the World

Tap a nearby block with a pickaxe selected to mine it — pickaxes fell trees, bamboo and coral too. Hold your finger to keep digging.

Tap an empty spot with a block selected to build.

⚔ button swings whatever you're holding at enemies.

Tap a cat to pet it. This is important.

Surviving the Night

When the sun sets, each biome breathes out its own spirits — dune shades in the desert, stalkers in the jungle, tide wisps at sea, hungry ghosts by the temples. Craft torches, build walls, or fight back.

Making Things

Open your pack 🎒 to see resources. Open craft ⚒ to turn wood, stone, sand and stranger things into tools, weapons, blocks and creature charms.

The Weapon Forge ✎ lets you draw your own weapon pixel-by-pixel — or import a picture — then forge it into the game for real.

The Four Realms

🏜 Sunken Dunes — cactus, sandstone, buried gold.

🌴 Verdant Tropics — your gentle starting home.

🌊 Glass Ocean — hold your breath, mind the depths, harvest coral.

🏮 Lantern Coast — warm winds, bamboo, golden temples, and cats. So many cats.

Saving

Mushmire saves itself as you play. Close the tab, come back — your empire remains.
